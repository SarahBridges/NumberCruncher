Mean

The mean of a set of numbers is the average. So we take the sum of all the numbers in the set and divide it by the number of numbers in the set.
The sum is 400 and there are 20 numbers in the set.
The mean is 20.

Mode

2, 2, 4, 6, 6, 7, 8, 11, 12, 18, 19, 20, 23, 24, 29, 31, 41, 43, 43, 50

The mode of a set of numbers is the the number (or numbers) that appear most often in the set.
There are three modes in this set of numbers and they each appear twice in the set.
The modes are 2, 6, and 43.

Median

2, 2, 4, 6, 6, 7, 8, 11, 12, 18, 19, 20, 23, 24, 29, 31, 41, 43, 43, 50

The median of a set of numbers is the value (or two values) which appear in the middle of the set, when the numbers are written in numerical order.
Because there are an even number of numbers in this set, there are two numbers that split the middle, so there are two medians.
The medians are 18 and 19.
